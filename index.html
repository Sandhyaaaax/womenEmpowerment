How HerShield Works

Your Safety, Automated.

Three simple steps and HerShield does the rest — monitoring, detecting, and alerting — so you never have to fumble in a crisis.

1

Quick Check-In

Set your expected safe duration before heading out. One tap and you're fully protected in the background.

2

Smart Monitoring

AI continuously tracks your location, motion, and device activity in the background. No interaction needed.

3

Automatic Alert

If no response when time ends, your trusted contacts are notified instantly with your live GPS location.

📍

Real-Time Safety Tracking

Set a timer before heading out. HerShield silently monitors your location. No response? It acts automatically on your behalf.

🧠

AI Distress Detection

On-device ML analyzes motion patterns and inactivity — even when you can't press a button. No audio is ever recorded.

🔔

Automatic Emergency Alert

Trusted contacts get notified with your exact GPS coordinates and movement history the moment something feels wrong.

🗺️

Community Safety Map

Crowd-sourced safety ratings — women mark and rate streets so others can travel with real, lived safety insights.

💬

Multilingual Wellness Chatbot

Talk in Hindi, Hinglish, or English — voice or text. Share how you feel; get support in the language you're most comfortable with.

🌸

Period & Health Tracker

Smart period reminders, PCOD guidance, breast health info, skincare community — one private, safe space for all women's health.
